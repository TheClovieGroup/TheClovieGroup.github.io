Challenge

Scattered emails and spreadsheets for space reservations, finance forms, vacation requests, and onboarding caused delays, rework, and missed details.

Solution

A centralized Airtable Help Directory with clear categories and standardized forms. Each card links to a live resource, cutting friction and missing info.

Results

  • 10+ processes in one hub
  • 100+ staff hours saved annually
  • Faster approvals & fewer follow-ups

Scope & Stack

  • Airtable bases, linked tables, and views
  • Form standardization & instructions
  • Light automations & notifications

No personal or student data is shown in this example. All private links have been redacted.
