Case Study

Request & Help Center using Airtable

100+ work hours saved through smarter reimbursements, orders, bookings, and onboarding.

Book a 20-minute consult See more projects

Challenge

The Dean’s Office depended on scattered emails and spreadsheets for space reservations, finance forms, vacation requests, and onboarding—causing delays and rework.

Solution

A centralized Airtable Help Directory with clear categories and standardized forms. Each card links to a live resource, reducing back-and-forth and missing info.

Results

  • 10+ processes in one hub
  • 100+ staff hours saved annually
  • Faster approvals & fewer follow-ups

Scope & Stack

  • Airtable bases, linked tables, views
  • Form standardization & instructions
  • Light automation & notifications

No personal or student data is shown in this example.

Client sector: Higher Education · Engagement: Operations & Workflow · Duration: 2–4 weeks
