The Clovie Group – Case Study

Request & Help Center using Airtable

100+ work hours saved: smarter reimbursements, orders, bookings, and onboarding.

Challenge

The Dean’s Office was using scattered emails and spreadsheets to manage space reservations, finance forms, vacation requests, and onboarding. Staff lost valuable time tracking links and following up on missing information.

Solution

I built a centralized Help Directory using Airtable. Each card links to a live form or resource — making it easy for staff to find what they need and reducing back-and-forth emails.

Results

  • 10+ processes centralized into one hub
  • 100+ hours saved annually across staff
  • Fewer email follow-ups and faster approvals
